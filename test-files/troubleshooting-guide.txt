Troubleshooting Guide

Common Issues and Solutions:

Issue: Documents not appearing after upload
Solution: Check that the ingestion process completed. Look for status
updates in the document list. If stuck in "processing", check backend logs.

Issue: Search returns no results
Solution: Ensure documents have been fully processed (status: ready).
Vector embeddings are generated during ingestion.

Issue: Authentication errors
Solution: Clear browser cookies and log in again. Check that your
session hasn't expired (sessions last 7 days).

Issue: Slow performance
Solution: Large documents may take longer to process. Consider breaking
very large files into smaller sections before upload.
